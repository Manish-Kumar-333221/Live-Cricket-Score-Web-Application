    • Which app is used for keeping track of cricket scores?

      Many cricket apps are used to track cricket scores and updates but Hindustan Times' riveting coverage is your go-to platform

    • How are live scores prepared in cricket?

      Live scores in cricket are prepared through a combination of immersive technology, stats, trivias and more. Centuries ago, when cricket was in its nascent phase, scores were tracked by noting the details down in books or merely pieces of paper. This practice is still followed in matches with no or limited coverage

    • What are some of the apps used to follow cricket live scores?

      Many cricket apps are used to track cricket scores and updates but Hindustan Times' riveting coverage is your new go-to platform

    • How is a cricket score sheet used?

      A cricket scoresheet has spaces available to note down the names of players and their performances. An old-school practice, it is still followed in school and several domestic matches

    • How do you determine cricket scores?

      Cricket scores are determined through runs and wickets, depending on which team or batter scores how many runs or takes wickets.
